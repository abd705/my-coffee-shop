Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are your opening hours?

    We’re open daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary — follow us on social media for updates.

  • Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

    Yes! We provide free high-speed Wi-Fi for all our customers.

  • Do you have dairy-free or vegan options?

    Absolutely. We offer soy, almond, and oat milk, plus a selection of vegan pastries.

  • Can I order online or for takeaway?

    Yes! You can order online through our website or order takeaway directly at the counter.

    Yes, delivery is available through selected delivery apps. Availability may depend on your location.

  • Do you sell whole coffee beans?

    Yes! We sell freshly roasted coffee beans. Ask our barista for recommendations.
